FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers.

How does Briefdeck work? expand_more
Briefdeck securely connects to your Google Calendar and Gmail. It analyzes your upcoming meetings and related emails to generate a concise pre-brief with context, insights, and suggested questions, delivered to your inbox 30 minutes before the call.
Is my data secure? expand_more
Yes, absolutely. We use industry-standard encryption and security protocols. Your data is used solely to generate your meeting briefs and is never sold or shared with third parties. We are working towards SOC2 compliance.
Does it work with Outlook or Teams? expand_more
Currently, we are optimized for the Google Workspace ecosystem (Calendar, Gmail, Meet). Support for Microsoft Outlook and Teams is on our roadmap and will be available soon.
Can I cancel my subscription anytime? expand_more
Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time from your account settings. You will continue to have access to Pro features until the end of your current billing period.
What is the difference between Free and Pro? expand_more
The Free plan gives you 40 pre-briefs per month and basic integrations. The Pro plan offers unlimited pre-briefs, real-time transcription, priority support, and access to our Chrome extension.
