Security at Briefdeck

Protecting your data is our top priority.

At Briefdeck, we take security seriously. Protecting your data is a core part of how we build and operate our product.

Data Encryption

All data transmitted between your browser and our servers is encrypted using HTTPS (TLS).

Sensitive information is stored securely and access is strictly limited.

Authentication & Access Control

Briefdeck uses secure OAuth authentication for third-party integrations such as Google.

We never store your third-party account passwords.

Access tokens are scoped to the minimum permissions required and can be revoked at any time from your provider’s security settings.

AI & Data Usage

Your data is used only to generate meeting briefs you request.

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties.

AI processing is limited strictly to the functionality of Briefdeck.

Data Storage & Retention

We store only the data necessary to operate the service.

Data is retained only as long as required for functionality.

You may request deletion of your data at any time.

Internal Access

Access to production systems is restricted to authorized team members only.

Administrative access is logged and reviewed.

Infrastructure & Monitoring

Briefdeck is hosted on secure cloud infrastructure.

Systems are monitored for reliability and unusual activity.

Regular updates and security patches are applied.

Responsible Disclosure

If you discover a security vulnerability, please report it responsibly by emailing:

support@briefdeck.tech

We appreciate the security community and take all reports seriously.
